The Origin

When Álvaro Siman started working with HOAs and condominiums across Broward, Miami-Dade, and Palm Beach counties, he noticed a pattern that no one was talking about: property managers, boards, and vendors were all working hard — but never together.

Maintenance requests got lost in email threads. Vendor histories lived inside someone's phone. Inspection notes disappeared after a board member resigned. Every property was, in a sense, starting from scratch every few years — with no institutional memory and no operational continuity.

The Siman Method™ was the answer. A structured operational framework — built not in a classroom, but across hundreds of real units, real buildings, and real problems in South Florida. A practical system that gave every property a shared language, a consistent process, and a record that survives beyond any single person.

Today, the Method is applied across 400+ units. And now, it's becoming a platform.

The Framework

The Siman Method™

A six-phase operational framework designed to bring structure, accountability, and continuity to any property — regardless of size, location, or management team.

01
Intake
Every request is received, classified by priority, and routed immediately. Nothing gets lost — residents get a confirmation, managers get visibility from minute one.
02
Assessment
Before any work starts, the property gets a full on-site evaluation. Issues are mapped, documented with photos, and an action plan is defined — no surprises mid-project.
03
Work Order
A structured work order is generated with scope, materials, timeline, and vendor assignment. Everyone knows exactly what needs to be done — and when.
04
Field Execution
Technicians execute with GPS check-in, photo documentation at every milestone, and real-time updates. Managers and boards can see progress without making a single phone call.
05
Quality Verification
Every completed job goes through a structured inspection checklist and supervisor sign-off before closure. The standard never drops — regardless of the job size.
06
Documentation & Closure
A full report is auto-generated — photos, timeline, costs, and sign-off — and delivered to the property manager. The property's history grows with every completed job.
